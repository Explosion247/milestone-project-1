Welcome

Welcome to The SteelBook Collection, this is a website you can use to find new steelbooks for your favourite games and keep track of your collection.

Description

Steelbooks are a common collectable that is often used to distinguish between different versions of a game. To try and get people to pay more for a game, companies will often include limited time version that will include different collectables. A steelbook is a metal verson of the case that the game will come in, these are often more durable and look better than the normal cases. However Steelbooks are used as a display item rather than a case for the game as they often include a normal case aswell.
