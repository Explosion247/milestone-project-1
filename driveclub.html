Daniel Deniz

The in-game sound system and also the cars sounds amazing. It even sometimes gives me shivers when I hear a v8 or v10... Game in controls is pretty good but, they could've added more manufacturers, it doesn't seem quite done with that. Also, the delays made this game not that popular than expected. I wish there was an option to test your cars without AI. But overall, this game would be very fun and competitive against other franchise. (Forza, Gran Turismo, Assetto Corsa)

Syed Hasan

Sad that this gem was discontinued in 2019. It's the perfect mix between arcade and simulation, the graphics are still amazing 7 years after release. The gameplay is great, with good physics. The water physics are the best I've seen in any game. Thank you evolution studios

Valter Kasuk

Amazing game for it's time. The only game that had self-reflections on the cars where you could see the reflection of side mirrors or wings on the car body. Great engine sounds! Good collection of cars. The rain effects are still incredible even by the 2021 standards! The minus for is the small amount of tracks and no real tracks.
